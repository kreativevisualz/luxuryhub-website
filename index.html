About Luxury Hub

At Luxury Hub, beauty is more than a look — it’s an experience. We believe every woman deserves to feel effortlessly confident, elegant, and seen. What began as a passion for luxury lash artistry has evolved into a modern beauty destination — where self-care meets sophistication.


Founded by Lebo Moloi, a visionary who understands the balance between student life, hustle, and the need to feel your best, Luxury Hub brings premium-quality services to everyday women who crave that polished, “put-together” glow.


With a touch of elegance in every detail, we combine professional artistry, quality products, and a client-first experience that leaves you feeling as good as you look. Whether it’s a fresh lash set, a hair install, or that final beauty touch before your big day — Luxury Hub is where confidence meets luxury.
